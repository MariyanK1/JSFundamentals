List exercise

HTML lists allow web developers to group a set of related items in lists.

Example:

    1.Brocolli
    This superfood is loaded with fiber, antioxidants to fight cancer, and vitamin C to aid in iron absorption.
    2.Carrots
    Carrots are an excellent source of vitamin A in the form of beta carotene. They are also a good source of several B vitamins, as well as vitamin K and potassium.
    3.Cauliflower
    Cauliflower is a cruciferous vegetable that is naturally high in fiber and B-vitamins. It provides antioxidants and phytonutrients that can protect against cancer. It also contains fiber to enhance weight loss and digestion, choline that is essential for learning and memory, and many other important nutrients.
